Stay organized

In Epic Projects, every project is divided in four sections : Brainstorming, Training, Assignments, Maintenance. This organization helps you to keep a clear vision of the project.

Epic Projects is built for individual projects, so there is no need for an internet connection. Thus, the app will be working 24/7. You can work whenever you want ;).
